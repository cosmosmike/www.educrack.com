ABOUT BANK EXAMS

Securing a Bank Job and getting placed in the Banking sector has been one of the most sought after career streams. To get into RBI, SBI & its associate banks, Nationalised Banks, Private sector banks, few International banks, and a large number of small to medium cooperative banks along with Gramin Banks, aspirants need to prepare for the banking exams conducted by the Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS), Reserve Bank of India (RBI), State Bank of India (SBI), and private banks. Exams like SBI PO, SBI Clerk, IBPS PO, IBPS Clerk, IBPS RRB, IBPS SO, RBI Grade B, RBI Assistant, etc. are conducted for recruitment in the banking sector.


IMPORTANCE

Each year, the Banks in India recruit over 1,00,000 candidates. This fact has generated an utter fascination for the youth in India towards the banking sector. The craze rises because of the handsome salary packages, job security and other additional perks and retirement benefits.

About IBPS RRB PO Exam

The IBPS RRB PO also known as Group ‘A’ Scale-I as per their advertisement. The exam is conducted to select deserving candidates for the role of officers in the banking sector. IBPS is responsible to conduct a complete selection process for the post and is conducted every year. Candidates looking to join the Banking sector as an officer shall apply for the post. Regional Rural Banks promise excellent career growth to the candidates who join as Scale – I officers. Their promotions are very fast and very soon if they perform well they can hold very critical positions of the banking sector.

The Exam Pattern

Stage 1 : Preliminary Exam
  • Reasoning (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 40)
  • Quantitative Aptitude (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 40)

Total test marks is 80 and The test total time is: 45 Mins

Stage 2 : Mains Exam
  • Reasoning (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 50)
  • Computer Knowledge (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 20)
  • General Awareness (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 40)
  • English Language (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 40)
  • Hindi Language (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 40)
  • Quantitative Aptitude (No of Questions 40)
    (Max. Marks 50)

Total test marks is 200 and The test total time is: 120 Mins


Penalty for wrong answers

In IBPS RRB PO exam there will be negative marking for each wrongly marked answers by the candidate. The negative marking will be equal to one fourth of the marks allotted to that particular question. No negative marks for unmarked responses.

Stage 3 : Interview

Candidates will be shortlisted based on the marks obtained in the Mains Examination and will be called to appear for Interview. Candidates are required to qualify the interview and 20% weightage will be given to marks obtained in it in the final merit list.

Stage 4 : Document Verification

Candidates qualified in Mains and interview will be called for the document verification process. If a candidate fails to produce requisite documents his/her candidature will be cancelled.

The Exam Dates

The notified schedule for IBPS RRB PO Preliminary Exam is in the month of Sep/Oct 2020. And the Mains of IBPS RRB PO will be held in Oct/Nov 2020. Candidates should visit the site for latest updates about call letters and exam dates. Due to this pandemic the schedule of the exam may be changed.

Exam Score

Marks obtained by candidates in the Mains exam are normalized by equipercentile formula and it will be used for shortlisting candidates for Interview.

The method is shown below :

Percentile Rank of Candidate =[(Total candidates in a shift )–(Rank of the candidate in that shift)] * 100 / (Total candidate in that shift -1)

The Score Validity

● After completion of Interview a final merit will be prepared by the board for different categories for provisional allotment.

● A reserved list will also be prepared up to certain extent of vacancies as decided by the board.

● The marks obtained in examination will be valid for one year from date of declaration of result.

● Reserved list candidates will be asked to join if any candidate from the main list fails to join.

Exam Eligibility

A candidate who wants to apply for IBPS RRB PO exam should meet the eligibility criteria as notified. The eligibility criteria for IBPS RRB PO is shown below :

● The age of candidate should be between 18 years to 30 years i.e. Candidates should be born between 03.07.1990 and later than 30.06.2002 (both dates inclusive). Relaxation is applicable to reserved category and ESM.

● Candidates should have completed a Bachelor's Degree in any stream from a recognized institute/university or its equivalent.

● Candidates should have proficiency in their Local Language.

● It is desirable that a candidate should have working knowledge of Computers.

Exam Fee

The Fee Structure for IBPS RRB PO Exam is as follows :

● For SC/ST/PWD/EXSM candidates – Rs. 175

● For all other candidates – Rs. 850

Fees once paid will be non refundable.

How to Apply

Official website : https://www.ibps.in/

Step 1: Open the official website of IBPS and click on Home Page.

Step 2: Open the link “CRP for RRBs” and then choose option “CLICK HERE TO APPLY ONLINE FOR CRP- RRBs-OFFICERS (Scale-I, II and III)” . The application form will appear on the screen.

Step 3: Provide your basic information and Click on “Click Here For New Registration” tab available on the page.

Step 4: The system will generate provisional Registration Number and Password. You will also receive an Email and SMS confirming the same.

Step 5: Candidates will have to upload their Photograph, Signature, Left hand thumb impression and a hand written declaration.

Step 6: Candidates are to use the ‘Save and Next’ option before finally submitting the application.

Step 7: You can preview your application and then finally click on the Submit button.

Step 8: Pay application fees online as per your category.

Step 9: You will receive a receipt confirming your payment. You can download it and keep it for future reference.

Benefits of taking the EXAM

The candidates who manage to clear IBPS RRB PO exam have a very good opportunity to work in banking field as an officer at an early age. The career is much more challenging but at the same time provides much more facilities to its employees. A person looking to make a full time career in officer cadre in banks must apply for the post. The salary provided by the bank is quite handsome apart from various allowances applicable from time to time. As the job is officer class so all top most facilities are provided to candidates selected for the post.
