A Brief Study About IIFT Exam

IIFT is a national-level management entrance exam conducted once in a year by the National Testing Agency (NTA), for admission to MBA (IB) program offered by Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) in New Delhi and Kolkata. It is a computer based test and evaluates a candidate’s aptitude in segments like quantitative ability, language comprehension, logical reasoning and general awareness. The test is conducted in a single session of two hours in objective answer format (multiple choice questions). The test is divided in four sections with a total number of questions changing each year. Last year the total number of questions was 110. The maximum marks for the test is 300 with varying sectional scoring patterns. There is a negative marking where 1/3 of the marks allotted for correct answer shall be deducted. The test was conducted by Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) before 2019 and is now conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

The Exam Pattern of IIFT

Earlier, the paper pattern for IIFT was slightly lengthy and it was a paper based test. However, the format has now been changed and reduced to 4 sections only.

(The time duration mentioned across each section is only for reference since the time spent on each section in open to individual candidate’s judgment and can be divided as per flexibility)

  • Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension (No of Questions 35)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • Quantitative Analysis (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (No of Questions 30)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • General Awareness (GK) (No of Questions 20)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)

Total test marks is 110 and The test total time is: 120 Mins

A candidate’s percentile is determined on the basis of where they stand in comparison to total number of students who attended the examination. It can hence only be estimated and never be accurately predicted. But we expect this estimation to be more or less accurate based on the previous year’s scores and cut offs.

THE SCORE VALIDITY-

The score validity refers to the amount of time IIFT credits stay valid after you’ve secured them. For IIFT, the validity is only one year. Meaning a candidate’s IIFT score expires after every year of scoring them. If the candidate fails to apply for admission to the college in that academic year, they will have to re-appear for the examinations. They will then apply for admission within that same academic year in which the new scores stand valid.

IIFT Exam Eligibility

For a candidate to appear for IIFT, they should clear certain criteria. The candidate should be an Indian citizen and must have a bachelor’s degree of minimum 3 years’ duration in any discipline from a recognized university or institution. The Applicant must have secured a minimum of 50% in graduation college. Candidates appearing for a qualifying examination can also apply subject to submission of proof of requisite qualification by beginning of October of the year of start of the course.(However, if the applicant graduates with less than the minimum required scores, their application with be automatically cancelled and they WILL NOT qualify to apply for colleges in that academic year) There is NO maximum age limit to apply for IIFT.

Exam Fee

The application fees for IIFT also includes the registration fees and need for be paid separately.

The application fees for boys, girls and transgender applicants belonging to general class is Rs.1650. For a candidate belonging to SC/ST/OBC and PWD (economically weak section, other backward cast, scheduled caste, scheduled tribes and persons with disabilities) the application fees is RS. 825 for both boys and girls.

Once applied, the registration cannot be cancelled and under circumstances where a student is unable to attend the exam, the application and registration fee WILL NOT be refunded.

How to Apply for the CMAT Exam?

STEP 1- Go to the official website of IIFT NTA. https://iift.nta.nic.in

STEP 2- Go to new registration and click on “fill application form”

STEP 3- Create your account (which you will use for all the proceedings of IIFT)

STEP 4- Once the account is verified, the website will take you to the registration page.

STEP 5- Carefully fill the basic details asked on the form including full name, residential/ permanent address, guardian’s name, date of birth, city, E-mail ID, password, and mobile number.

STEP 6- Submit the information after checking.

STEP 7- You will receive your application number.

STEP 8- Enter the details and past academic records mentioned in the application.

STEP 9- Check the form carefully and then submit. (form once submitted cannot be edited)

STEP 10- Upload scanned passport size photo and Signature.

STEP 11- Choose the preferred exam centre from the provided list.

STEP 12- Pay the application fee online through debit/credit card, net banking and UPI/PAYTM services. (the application fee can only be paid online) (check above for detailed information on the fee amount)

STEP 13- Wait for your application confirmation page and then save a copy of the page. (download).

Benefits of taking the EXAM

IIFT is one of the most preferred and recommended exams to attend in order to attend a good and reputed college i.e. the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade.

The cut off for the college is slightly lesser than other examinations. It is one of the leading management colleges in the country.

IIFT, unlike the IIM’S is relatively lower on the fees and hence assures a better return of investment through great placements at this college.

IIFT is considered to be moderately difficult and gives you access to quality education. Especially for a candidate appearing for other management entrance examinations like CET and CAT, applying to IIFT can increase the chances and the syllabus is almost the same as the other exams.
