A Brief Study About CMAT Exam

CMAT stands for the common management entrance exam. This is a national level aptitude-based entrance examination that lets you apply to management institutions across India. CMAT is a computer-based test and evaluates a candidate's aptitude in segments like quantitative ability, language comprehension, logical reasoning, and general awareness. The test is conducted in a single session of three hours in objective answer format (multiple choice questions). The test is divided into four sections and each one consists of 25 questions. For each correct question, the candidate secures 4 marks, and four every wrong answer 1 mark is deducted. The maximum marks for the test is 400.

The test was conducted by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) before 2018 and is now conducted by NTA (National testing agency). The CMAT scores are considered by all colleges/institutions /universities approved by AICTE.

The Exam Pattern of CMAT

The paper pattern for CMAT is slightly simpler than other examinations because each section is given equal weightage in terms of marks.

(The time duration mentioned across each section is only for reference since the time spent on each section is open to individual candidate's judgment and can be divided as per flexibility)

  • Quantitative techniques and data interpretation (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 45 Mins)
  • Logical reasoning (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 45 Mins)
  • General awareness (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 45 Mins)
  • Language comprehension (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 45 Mins)

The test total time is: 3 hours

THE SCORE VALIDITY-

The score validity refers to the amount of time CMAT credits stay valid after you've secured them. For CMAT, validity is only one year. Meaning a candidate's CMAT score expires after a year of scoring them. If the candidate fails to apply to colleges or is not satisfied with the colleges they are eligible for in that academic year, they will have to re-appear for the examinations. They will then apply to colleges within that same academic year in which the new scores stand valid.

CMAT Exam Eligibility

For a candidate to appear for CMAT, they should clear certain criteria. Candidate should be an Indian citizen and should have an education of a minimum of fifteen years including having graduated in any discipline from a recognized university or institution. The Applicant must have secured a minimum of 50% in graduation college. For applicants from sc/st/pwd category, the minimum eligibility score in graduation is 45%.

The final year graduation students who expect their results to be declared before the commencement of admission can also apply for the examination. (However, if the applicant graduates with less than the minimum required scores, their application with be automatically canceled and they WILL NOT qualify to apply for colleges in that academic year.)

There is NO maximum age limit to apply for CMAT.

Exam Fee

The application fees for CMAT also include the registration fees and need to be paid separately.

The application fees for boys belonging to the general class is Rs.1600 and for a girl belonging to the general class is Rs.1000. For a candidate belonging to EWS/OBC-NCL (economically weak section, other backward castes, and non-creamy layer) the application fee is RS. 1000 for both boys and girls.

Candidates belonging from SC/ST and PWD (scheduled caste, scheduled tribes, and persons with disabilities) have to pay a fee of Rs.800 for boys and Rs.700 for girls.

For Transgender applicants, the application fee is Rs.700.

Once applied, the registration cannot be canceled and under circumstances where a student is unable to attend the exam, the application and registration fee WILL NOT be refunded.

How to Apply for the CMAT Exam?

STEP 1- Go to the official website of CMAT NTA. https://cmat.nta.nic.in/webinfo/public/home.aspx

STEP 2- Go to the new registration and click on "fill application form"

STEP 3- Create your account (which you will use for all the proceedings of GMAT)

STEP 4- Once the account is verified, the website will take you to the registration page.

STEP 5- Carefully fill the basic details asked on the form including full name, residential/ permanent address, guardian’s name, date of birth, city, E-mail ID, password, and mobile number.

STEP 6- Submit the information after checking.

STEP 7- You will receive your 12- digit application number.

STEP 8- Enter the details and past academic records mentioned in the application.

STEP 9- Check the form carefully and then submit. (form once submitted cannot be edited)

STEP 10- Upload scanned passport size photo and Signature.

STEP 11- Pay the application fee online through debit/credit card, net banking, and UPI/PAYTM services. (the application fee can only be paid online) (check above for detailed information on the fee amount)

STEP 12- Wait for your application confirmation page and then save a copy of the page. (download).

Benefits of taking the EXAM

CMAT is one of the most preferred and recommended exams to attend to spike up your chances of landing in a good and reputed college.

Over 500 great colleges including JBIMS, SIMSREE, PUMBA, K J Somaiya, which are one of the top placement providing colleges in Maharashtra accept CMAT scores. The cut off’s for these colleges through CMAT is slightly lesser than other examinations. So if you have your eyes set on any of the top colleges, attending CMAT will increase your chances of actually making it.

CMAT only places candidates to AICTE approved colleges and applies to institutions on a national level. CMAT assures admission in both MBA and PGDM courses in these colleges.

CMAT, unlike the IIM’S place you to colleges that are relatively lower on the fees and hence assure a better return of investment through great placements in these colleges.

CMAT Is considered to be moderately difficult and gives access to a lot of top colleges across states India. Especially for a candidate appearing for other management entrance examinations like CET and CAT, applying to CMAT can increase the chances and the syllabus is almost the same as the other exams.

Career Scope after CMAT Exam

After graduating from any business college that CMAT places you in, the options are endless. Management professionals are always at the top of the market. They have various industries lined for a career option. In the banking sector or the finance sector, even in the hospitality sector, there are endless jobs for management professionals. Ranging from business operations and relationship management to wealth management and risk analytics, management professionals have too many options they can choose from after considering their caliber and interest and placement options at the time of graduation.
