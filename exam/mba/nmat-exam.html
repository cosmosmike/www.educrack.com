A Brief Study About NMAT 2020 Exam

It is an online computer-based test on the pattern of CAT, XAT, SNAP with only difference in the number of questions and difficulty level. It is a moderate difficulty level exam with 108 questions divided into three core sections of Verbal Ability, Quant, and Reasoning. The duration of the exam is 2 hours.

NMAT is a National-level MBA entrance test for admissions in NMIMS University and other reputed B-Schools in India. Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is a top-ranked (deemed) university offering UG, PG and Ph.D. level courses in more than 10 streams and their specializations.

This exam is a candidate friendly exam. It is one of the most important but a moderate MBA entrance exam which was conducted by the Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC).

Online CBT and MCQ Format:

The NMAT 2020 exam pattern is an online computer-based test and the questions in the NMAT 2020 exam pattern are of Multiple Choice type (MCQs).

Total questions: In 2020 NMAT exam has a total of 108 questions. Earlier the exam had 120 questions.

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The NMAT exam is a moderate difficulty level test, divided into three sections without any negative marking for wrong answers.

Your Final scorecard will include a scaled score for each section, a total score as well as a percentile ranking. The following table lists the range of scores possible on the different sections by the GMAC exam.

Types of Questions Score Range
Quantitative Skills 12-120
Language Skills 12-120
Logical Reasoning skills 12-120
Total 36-360

Final Percentile Rank

The final percentile rankings will be calculated after all the candidates have taken their exam(s) and will be announced and updated in your scorecard in the 3rd week of January 2021.

NMAT Exam Eligibility

The applicants are required to fulfill the below-mentioned eligibility criteria for appearing in the NMAT Exam 2020.

● The candidate must hold a Bachelor's Degree from a Government Recognized University.

● A minimum of 50% of marks is required to be scored in the graduation degree program.

● Candidates in the final year of Graduation are also eligible to apply for NMAT by GMAC 2020.

● No work Experience is Required for this Exam

● There are No Age Restrictions for the Candidates who are appearing NMAT Exams.

● Candidates can attempt this exam three times.

Candidates are advised to check their eligibility before applying. If they are found ineligible or involved in any unfair activities, their application is to be canceled at any stage of the selection process for admission.

Exam Fee

Candidates have to pay the requisite application fee before the mentioned deadline and have to submit the application form along with the required documents.

NMAT by GMAC registration fee is announced every year by GMAC before the commencement of registration process.

How to Apply for the NMAT Exam?

Step 1: Visit the official website of NMAT by GMAC i.e. (nmat.org.in.)

Step 2: Create an account with a valid email address.

Step 3: Provide contact information, personal, educational, and work experience, the list of programs to send scores, and the ID that you will use at the test center.

Step 4: Select schools where you wish to send your result. This will be under the "Programme Details" section.

Step 5: Select up to 7 programs that are included in their registration fee. An additional fee of INR 200 + applicable taxes is applicable for each program added over and above 7 programs.

6: The photograph must have a resemblance to the candidate and the photograph on the primary ID. This photograph is included on the admit card.

Step 7: Pay the registration fee 2000 + taxes.
