A Brief Study About XAT Exam

Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) , is one of the largest MBA Entrance exams for admission in XLRI, SPJIMR, IMT, XIMB, TAPMI and more than 150 colleges in India. It is a national level entrance exam conducted once in a year. This exam is conducted to choose the students fairly for admission according to the available seats in college. It is mainly conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur, therefore it is called XLRI – XAT. The students for admission are chosen according to the marks they scored in the XAT exam.

The Exam Pattern of XAT Exam

Part 1 :
  • Verbal and Logical Ability (No of Questions 26)
    (Time Duration 55 Mins)
  • Decision making (No of Questions 21)
    (Time Duration 55 Mins)
  • Quantitative ability and data interpretation (No of Questions 28)
    (Time Duration 55 Mins)

The test total time is: 165 Mins


Part 2 :
  • General Knowledge (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 15 Mins)

The test total time is: 15 Mins


XAT Exam pattern and syllabus 2021

The XAT exam pattern for 2021 has numerous changes in it. The 2021 exam pattern is much easier than the previous years. XLRI bought many changes in numbers and composition syllabus from XAT exam pattern. Apart from removing essay writing tests from exam paper patterns. New types of NAT question papers are introduced in XAT exams for students. To cover the new XAT exam pattern and score good marks, candidates should follow the new rules of XAT 2021 exam pattern. Following are the features and changes in the XAT exam pattern 2021 according to each syllabus :

1. VARC & LR 1ST SECTION :

The XAT exam pattern of 2021 for Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) and Logical Reasoning (LR) is :

● Number of questions : 26 ● Types of questions : MCQs ● RC passages : 4 ● RC based questions :14 to 16 ● Number of VA questions : 8 to 10 ● VA syllabus : Vocabulary, Analogy, Para jumbles, Para competition, Critical Reasoning, Statement and Assumptions ● Lod : Moderate to very difficult.
2. Decision Making 2nd Section :

XLRI exam pattern of Decision Making decides the fate of candidates. The questions in the Decision making section are like small caselets and decision making questions. These questions are mainly in sets.

● Number of questions : 21 ● Types of questions : MCQs ● Questions : 7 to 8 sets of decision making problems followed by 2 and 3 questions each ● LOD : Moderate to very difficult
3. Quantitative Ability and Data Interpretation 3rd section :

XAT exam pattern of Quantitative ability and data interpretation consist of all types of questions from quantitative ability and data interpretation. The sets of questions consist of 3-4

● Number of questions : 28 ● Types of questions : MCQs ● LoD : Moderate to difficult ● Questions frequently asked in Quantitative ability and Data interpretation : The questions are frequently asked on topics like Time & Work, profit & loss, mensuration, ven diagram, bar diagram, percentage, equation, number system, clock, geometry, algebra, table, graph.
4. General Knowledge 4th section :

There is no XAT syllabus or pattern for G. K. section in XAT. Anything under the sun can be asked in G. K. section

● Number of questions : 25 ● Types of questions : MCQs ● Time : 15 minutes ● LoD : Moderate to difficult

Topic : GK questions based on any topic including Current domestic and International affairs, Business, Economic, Social issues, appointments, conventional GK, sports, Constitution of India among others.

Note : Marks of GK are not calculated during the calculation of XAT percentage.

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The score validity of the XAT exam is one year. You can get admission in any college for MBA till one year with the marks you scored in your XAT exam. Once the date expires then you have to re – appear for XAT exams for the new certificate to get admission.

XAT 2021 Exam Eligibility

The candidates who are appearing for XAT exams should have a Bachelor’s degree or should be in the last year of bachelor’s degree from a recognized college or Institute. The age of the candidates should be from 18 years. Younger than 18 are not allowed to appear for XAT exams.

Exam Fee

XAT exams 2021 First phase application fee : Rs. 1700 (Rs.300 per section)

XAT exams 2021 Late application fee : Rs. 2000 (Rs.500 per section)

● Cancellation Fee : There are no cancellation fees neither the paid fees is refundable. It is clearly mentioned in the form that fees once paid is not refundable.

How to Apply for the XAT Exam?

Demand draft facility is still available. You just have to pay the fees for the XAT exam and send a demand draft to the admission office. Once the date is passed for admission then you have to pay extra fees as late charge.

Benefits of XAT

Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) is a national level entrance exam conducted by XLRI, Jamshedpur for admission to MBA/PGDM programmes. Apart from XLRI and 10 other XAMI members, over 700 B – schools accept XAT scores for admission of the students in different management courses.

Career Scope after XAT Exam

Candidates who applied for XAT examination and passed can apply for MBA institutes in all over India. Candidates can only apply in institutes where XAT marks are acceptable for admission.
