About SSC CGL Exam

Staff Selection Commission – Combined Graduate Level Examination SSC CGL is an examination conducted to recruit staff on posts like Assistant Audit Officer, Assistant Account Officer, Assistant Section Officer, Assistant, Inspector of Income Tax, Inspector (Central Excise), Assistant Enforcement Officer, Sub Inspector, Inspector, Junior Statistical Inspector, Statistical Officer Grade – 2, Auditor, Accountant/Junior Accountant, Senior Secretariat Assistant/Upper Division Clerks and Tax Assistant for the Government of India.

The Exam Pattern

Tier 1 :
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 15 Mins)
  • General Awareness (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 15 Mins)
  • English Comprehension (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 15 Mins)
  • Quantitative Aptitude (No of Questions 25)
    (Time Duration 15 Mins)

The test total time is: 60 Mins


Tier 2 :
  • Quantitative Ability (No of Questions 100)
    (Time Duration 2 Hours)
  • English language and Comprehension (No of Questions 200)
    (Time Duration 2 Hours)
  • Statistics (No of Questions 100)
    (Time Duration 2 Hours)
  • General Studies (Finance and Economics) (No of Questions 100)
    (Time Duration 2 Hours)

The test total time is: 8 Hours


Tier 3 :
  • Descriptive paper in English/Hindi (Writing of Essay, Precise, Letter, Application, etc.) (No of Questions 1)
    (Time Duration 60 Mins)

The test total time is: 60 Mins


Trial 4 :

SSC CGL Tier 4 is a computer skill test. It will be conducted in two steps DEST Test and CPT Test

DEST Test

Candidates are required to type 2000 words in 15 minutes in English on a computer. This test is conducted to check the writing skills of the candidate.

CPT Test

This check is conducted to check a candidate’s proficiency in Word Processing, Spreadsheet and Generation of Slides.

The Exam Dates

The Exam Date of Tier 1 is 3 – 9 March, 2020.

Tier 2 is 14 – 17 October, 2020

Tier 3 is 1 November, 2020

The date of Tier 4 is not yet notified by the Government of India.

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The score validity of SSC CGL exam is of one year. The marks you scored in your exam are valid for 1 year to get a job. You can apply anywhere according to your post you applied for exam, for 1 year with that score. Once the score validity expires you have to attempt the exam for one more time the next year.

Exam Eligibility

To apply for the SSC CGL exam the candidate should have Graduated or must have Bachelor’s Degree of any subject from a recognized University/Institution. And the most important thing, that the candidate must have studied Mathematics/Statistics/Economics as a compulsive or as an elective subject.

Exam Fee

● Cancellation Fee: The cancellation charges are zero.

● GATE Rescheduling Fee: Candidates with a Bachelor’s Degree are eligible to apply for SSC CGL examination. The SSC CGL examination fees is Rs. 100/- . However, Women, SC, ST, PWD, Ex-servicemen are exempted from fee payment.

How to Apply

Official website : https://www.ssc.nic.in/

Step 1: Open the official website of SSC CGL examination ssc.nic.in.

Step 2: Click on the ‘Register now’ option displayed on the screen.

Step 3: Fill your personal details in the form which are asked like Name, Date of Birth, Mobile number, Email ID, Educational Background, etc.

Step 4: Upload your passport size photo and signature in the given columns.

Step 5: Click on the ‘Register’ button below the form.

Step 6: Pay SSC CGL application fee.
