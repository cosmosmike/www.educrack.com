About SSC MTS Exam

The Staff Selection Commission conducts SSC MTS exam every year to select candidates for various class ‘c’ posts in different Govt. Departments. It is one of the most famous recruitment and each year a huge number of aspirants appear for the exam but only few could make it into success as the competition is very tough. SSC MTS exam gives a great opportunity to 10th pass candidates to secure a Government Job. The multitasking staff provides diversified job profiles to its candidates. A person selected through this exam may be given any type of general responsibility and work as per requirement of the organization.

The Exam Pattern

Stage 1 : Tier I Exam (Objective type)
  • General English (No of Questions 25)
    (Max. Marks 25)
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning (No of Questions 25)
    (Max. Marks 25)
  • Numerical Aptitude (No of Questions 25)
    (Max. Marks 25)
  • General Awareness (No of Questions 25)
    (Max. Marks 25)

Total test marks is 100 and The test total time is: 90 Mins


Note:

90 Minutes time will be given to complete all sections of the paper,120 Minutes for candidates eligible for Scribes.


Stage 2 : Tier II Exam (Descriptive type)
  • Candidate needs to write a short Essay or letter in English or any other language included in the 8th Schedule of the Constitution (Max. Marks 50) 30 Minutes time will be given to finish the paper, 40 Minutes for candidates eligible for scribes.

Penalty for wrong answers

As the paper one is of objective type so there will be negative marking in that paper only. The penalty will be 0.25 marks for each wrong response in the paper. If no response is marked by the candidate then there is no penalty.

Stage 3 : Document Verification

Candidates who are selected after both Tier I & Tier II of SSC MTS exam will be called for verification of documents. Candidates are to positively produce their relevant certificates and other documents otherwise their candidature may also be cancelled.

The Exam Dates

Due to the pandemic the official notification for SSC MTS exam is yet to be published. As per the yearly calendar of SSC the exams are likely to be conducted in the month of Oct/Nov 2020. The candidates are advised to remain updated by visiting the official website for any further notification about the exam.

The Score Validity

● After successful completion of all steps a final merit list will be prepared by SSC.

● A main list and a S/By list will be prepared by SSC.

● The marks obtained in the exam are valid for one year only from the date of result declaration.

● S/By candidates may be asked to join in case Main List candidates do not join for any reason.

Exam Eligibility

Candidates who decide to apply for SSC MTS exam are to fulfill certain eligibility criteria which is as follows:

● The age limit is divided into two groups which are 18 to 25 years and 18 to 27 years as per post and department.

● Candidate should be 10th pass or equivalent from recognized board.

Exam Fee

The fee charged for SSC MTS Exam is as follows:

● For General category candidates – Rs. 100

● Women candidates, SC, ST, Pwd, ESM are need not to pay any exam fee

The exam fee is non refundable.

How to Apply

Official website : https://www.ssc.nic.in/

Step 1: For the first time user you have to complete one time registration.

Step 2: Open the official website and click on the link “one time registration”.

Step 3: Fill in basic details like contact details, upload scanned ../../images of the passport size photo and the signature.

Step 4: Now login with your registration number and password generated by One time registration process.

Step 5: Click Apply link in “Multitasking Examination 2020”

Step 6: Fill in all other required details and click submit to proceed for payment.

Step 7: Pay your application fee online by any method including debit or credit card, UPI, Netbanking, etc.

Step 8: On successful submission of the online application take a print out of the application for future reference.

Benefits of taking the EXAM

SSC MTS is one of the most famous exams across all competitive examinations. This exam gives an entry level post to a candidate in Govt. Sector and provides a secured career. Candidates once get into departments may apply for departmental exams and improve their career prospects. The salary is good and various allowances are paid which are applicable to Govt. Employees like HRA, DA, TPT etc. So those candidates who want to secure an entry level job in Govt. sector should apply for it.

Career Scope after Exam

SSC MTS is a good job for those who have successfully completed Class 10th. After recruitment, successful candidates will get Rs 16,000–20,000 in hand salary, along with all the perks, bonuses, and additional allowances as mentioned in 7th Pay Commission.

The candidates working as multitasking staff are also entitled to various types of increments under the Pay Band I scheme.
