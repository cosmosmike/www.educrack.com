About RRB NTPC Exam

RRB NTPC Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) conducts Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) exam to recruit commercial apprentices, Good guard, Traffic apprentice, Traffic Assistant, Assistant Station Master, etc. all over India. It is a national level entrance exam conducted once a year when the vacancies for the following posts are available.

This exam is conducted and checked by the government bodies and the posts for which the exams are conducted are also government posts. Many people apply for this exam. RRB NTPC exam is conducted all over India at the same time. In order to work in the government firms, you have to pass the exam for that.

The Exam Pattern

The RRB NTPC exam pattern is in 4 stages
● Trial 1 Computer Based Test (CBT) ● Trial 2 Computer Based Test (CBT) ● Typing skills/Computer Based Aptitude Test ● Documents verification/Medical Examination
Trial 1 :
  • Mathematics (No of Questions 30)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning (No of Questions 30)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • General Awareness (No of Questions 40)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)

The test total time is: 90 Mins


Trial 2 :
  • Mathematics (No of Questions 35)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning (No of Questions 35)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)
  • General Awareness (No of Questions 50)
    (Time Duration 30 Mins)

The test total time is: 90 Mins


Trial 3 :
RRB NTPC Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT) :

(It is only for the candidates who appeared for Station Master and Traffic Assistant)

● The candidates should secure a minimum T-score of 42 in each of the test batteries to qualify. ● This is applicable for all candidates irrespective of community and category and no relaxation in T-score is permissible. ● Candidates have to qualify in each of the test batteries of CBAT for SM/TA. ● The CBAT will have question and answer options only in English and Hindi language. ● There is no negative marking in CBAT. ● The Merit list of CBAT candidates will be drawn according to the 70% marks weightage of 2nd Trial and 30% marks weightage of CBAT
Typing Skills Test (TST) ● Qualifying in nature ● The number of candidates called will be 8 times more than the number of vacancies. ● The candidates should be able to type 30 words per minute in English and 25 words per minute in Hindi, in their personal computer, but this time only without using any Editing Or spell checking tool.
Trial 4 :
Documents Verification (DV) :

Based on the marks of the candidates in 2nd CBT and CBAT/TST (as applicable) the candidates will be called equal to the number of vacancies for the documents verification.

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The score validity of the following exam is of one year. For one year you can apply anywhere for the job you applied for in the exam. But after one year the score validity expires and the candidates have to apply for the exam once more for a new score. Once the score validity expires then no institutions accept the score card or the exam.

Exam Eligibility

For the RRB NTPC exam, the candidates have completed undergraduate (10+2) exams successfully. If the candidates do not have the (10+2) qualification, then they cannot apply for the following examination. The minimum age required for the RRB NTPC exam is 18 years and the maximum age requirement is 30 years. Candidates younger than 18 and older than 30 cannot apply for RRB NTPC examination.

Exam Fee

To appear for RRB NTPC examination the fees for General and OBC category is Rs. 500/- whereas, it is Rs. 250/- for SC/ST/PWD/Women/Ex-servicemen/Transgender/Minority and Economically Backward Classes.

There are zero cancellation charges for the exam and the refund for Minority and Economically Backward Classes is Rs. 250 and for the rest of the class is Rs. 400

How to Apply

Official website : https://www.indianrailways.gov.in/

Step 1: Visit the official website.

Step 2: Click on recruitment for NTPC, paramedical level 1 and MI posts.

Step 3: Fill the required details

Step 4: Upload the scanned photo and signature

Step 5: Submit the application and pay the fees
