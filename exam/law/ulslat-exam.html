A Brief Study About ULSAT Exam

ULSAT is a University level entrance exam which is conducted by universities for admission in BA LLB (Hons.) / BBA LLB (Hons.) /B.com LLB (Hons.) /LLM courses offered by UPES. Candidates are shortlisted for the admission on the basis of their performance in entrance exam, group discussion and personal interview. This exam is conducted to get fair admission for all the students and to test the ability of the students.

The Exam Pattern of ULSAT

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The score validity of the ULSAT examination is of one year. By this particular score you scored in your exam, you can apply for admission in any law colleges. Universities will accept your application with a current score only for one year. Once the duration expires, candidates have to reappear for the examination.

Exam Fee

The application fees of ULSAT is 1350/-. Candidates have to pay the amount online through net banking, debit or credit card. Candidates can print receipts for future purposes. The application fees is non refundable after cancellation.

● Cancellation Fee: Cancellation charges are zero
