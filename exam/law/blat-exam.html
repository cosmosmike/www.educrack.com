A Brief Study About BLAT Exam

BLAT is an entrance examination conducted by the Banaras Hindu University (BHU) for admitting the students into its 5-year BA LLB course. This exam is conducted every year and the same for the year 2020 is scheduled to be between 16th – 31st August. The minimum number of admission seats are 30 and the maximum is 60.

The students aspiring to pursue law education can give this exam online as well as offline. The pen and paper-based BLAT exam is held in 45 different cities and the online exam will be conducted in 115 different cities in India. BLAT exam is based on the MCQ pattern and there are 150 questions in the exam. The registrations for the BLAT 2020 have already been closed. If you want to know about the eligibility, exam pattern, steps to fill the form and also the career scope for the same in future then read the below-stated information.

The Exam Pattern of BLAT

The BLAT is an MCQ based exam which can be taken online as well as offline. The total number of questions in the exam is 150 and the time given to attempt the exam is 120 minutes. Students should note that no extra time would be given to them. Marks for every right question is “3” and “1” mark would be deducted for every wrong answer. The maximum number of marks for the exam is 450. The exam is divided into 4 sections- English/Hindi comprehension, General Awareness & Current Affairs, Aptitude & Mental Ability and Common Legal Knowledge. This BLAT exam is conducted in both mediums- Hindi as well as English.

  • English/Hindi Comprehension
  • General Awareness & Current Affairs
  • Aptitude & Mental Ability
  • Common Legal Knwoledge

The test total time is: 2 hours

The Exam Dates

The registration for the BLAT exam had begun on 30th January 2020 and the last date to apply for the same was 12th March 2020. The last chance for the students who wish to change their BLAT exam centre was on 20th June 2020. The tentative examination date is between 16th-31st August 2020. The date on which admit cards will be available has not been announced yet. We advise students to keep checking the official website for Banaras Hindu University. THE SCORE VALIDITY

The validity of the score of the BLAT entrance exam is just for one year. If the student does not enrol in the study program of LAW in one year then he/she will be required to clear the entrance exam of BLAT again to secure admission in LAW in Banaras Hindu University.

BLAT Exam Eligibility

The students who want to give the BLAT exam should have completed 10+2 from a recognized school with a minimum 50% aggregate for the General category whereas the same for OBC is 45% and for SC/ST is 40%. As such there is no age restriction for the exam by the Supreme Court of India however the person should have completed his/her education criterion. A student can appear for this examination as many time as he/she wishes for and this is conducted one in a year.

Exam Fee

The registration fee for the BHU’s BLAT exam is INR 600 for the General Category and INR 300 for teh SC/ST. There is no cancellation fee charged by BHU. Given the fact that the application process for the BLAT exam is done online, one can easily pay the fee via online as many different payment gateways are available.

How to Apply for the CLAT Exam?

To apply for the exam, candidates can visit the official website - http://bhuonline.in/

Step 1: You have to visit the official website of the Banaras Hindu University to start your registration process for BLAT (http://bhuonline.in/)

Step 2: Click on the link for BLAT entrance exam and then further select the register after filling the required details.

Step 3: Now you will be asked to login to your account on the official website with the user ID and password you had just created.

Step 4: Before you begin to fill the form further please keep your educational certificates, passport-size photo and other address proof.

Step 5: Keep the card ready for making the payment for your convenience.

Step 6: Keep following the instructions and start uploading all the necessary documents and filling in every information properly.

Step 7: Once you have filled and uploaded every detail please re-check the information carefully.

Step 8: Go to the payment portal and make the payment. After successful payment, an invoice will be generated.

Step 9: Click on the done option. Now you have successfully registered for your BLAT entrance examination. Make sure to keep the application form and the fee receipt copies for future reference.

Benefits of taking the EXAM

Every year thousands of students register themselves for the entrance exam of BLAT conducted by the Banaras Hindu University to secure a seat in its 5-year BA LLB Program. The students who come to give the exam are from different places.

BHU is known to be one of the best universities to pursue law education. The university not only focuses on the academic development of its students but also the overall personality development which will help them to become highly employable in the future.

Career Scope after Exam

This 5-year integrated BA LLB Program form BHU opens up so many opportunities for its passing students. If a student wishes to secure a government job, then after this course is completed he/she becomes eligible to apply for the law-related government jobs.

On the other hand, students can also secure a job in the leading companies across the world as a litigator, corporate lawyer, law consultant or even practice on their own.
